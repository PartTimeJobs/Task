Buy and sell domain names

A domain name is just a website address (such as "savethestudent.org" or "mysite.co.uk") and has many extensions (.com, .net, .co.uk, etc.).

Registered on GoDaddy.com, their price is only 0.99 US dollars, and the price of high-quality domain names can even reach millions of dollars when they are sold. In 2007, the sales price of VacationRentals.com was as high as 35 million US dollars!

You may not encounter similar problems now, but you can still make quick profits with a little search. The trick is to find an available domain name with certain commercial value, make a snap purchase, and then list the domain name for sale on websites such as Sedo.com.
